    • Welcome to Eventh Privacy Policy

    Welcome to Eventh Privacy Policy. At Eventh, your privacy is very important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information at all times. When you register for our events, book services, or interact with our website, we may collect information such as your name, email, phone number, payment details, and event preferences.

    This information is used solely to provide seamless event experiences, communicate important updates, process payments securely, and offer personalized support. We never sell your data, and it is stored safely using modern security measures. By using our services, you trust us to handle your information responsibly and transparently.

    1. Information We Collect

    We collect personal details including your name, email address, phone number, and payment information when you register for events, subscribe to updates, or fill out booking forms. Additionally, we may gather feedback, survey responses, and preferences to enhance our services. This information is always collected with your consent and used solely for professional purposes related to event management. Our goal is to understand your needs and provide a personalized experience, ensuring that your interactions with Eventh are smooth, secure, and enjoyable while protecting your privacy and data at every step.

    2. How We Use Your Information

    Your information allows us to communicate event details, send reminders, process payments safely, and provide prompt customer support. We may also use your data to inform you about upcoming events, promotions, or newsletters that may interest you. Every piece of information is handled responsibly and with care to maintain your trust. Our team ensures that personal information is only accessed by authorized personnel for professional purposes, and we take all necessary steps to protect it from unauthorized use. This enables us to deliver high-quality services tailored to your preferences efficiently and securely.

    3. Sharing with Third Parties

    We do not sell your personal information to third parties under any circumstances. However, in certain cases, we may share limited details with trusted partners such as event venues, catering providers, or payment gateways to successfully manage your events.

    These partners are required to keep your data safe and use it solely for the purposes agreed upon. Sharing is done responsibly, and we ensure that your privacy is always respected. We continuously monitor these relationships to maintain compliance and safeguard your personal information throughout the event management process.

    4. Payment & Security

    All online transactions on our website are processed through secure payment gateways with strong encryption to protect your financial information. We also apply industry-standard security practices, including SSL certificates and regular system monitoring, to prevent unauthorized access. Our team is committed to maintaining the confidentiality of your personal data and ensuring that your payment and event information remain secure at all times. By implementing these measures, we provide you with confidence and peace of mind while interacting with our website and making transactions, keeping your data safe and protected.

    5. Cookies & Website Use

    Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience, remember your preferences, and analyze visitor behavior to improve site performance. Cookies help us provide personalized services, track usage patterns, and ensure that the site operates efficiently. You can disable cookies in your browser if you prefer, but some website features may not function properly without them. By allowing cookies, you help us understand user needs better and continuously improve the quality of our website, ensuring that every visit is smooth, intuitive, and tailored to your preferences, while still keeping your privacy in mind.

    • Improve your browsing experience
    • Remember your preferences
    • Analyze visitor behavior to enhance site performance

    6. Data Protection

    We take your privacy seriously and implement strict measures to protect your personal information from misuse, unauthorized access, or disclosure. Our systems are regularly updated, monitored, and maintained to ensure the highest level of security. Personal data is stored securely and is only accessible to authorized personnel for legitimate purposes. These measures include encryption, secure storage, and controlled access. By following these practices, we ensure that your information is handled responsibly, giving you confidence that your privacy is safeguarded while interacting with Eventh services and that your data is protected at every stage of processing.

    7. Your Rights

    You have the right to request access to your personal data, update incorrect information, or ask for deletion of your details at any time. If you no longer wish to receive marketing messages or newsletters, you can unsubscribe easily. We respect your choices and ensure that your rights are upheld under applicable privacy laws. Our team is always available to assist you with any requests regarding your data. By exercising these rights, you can maintain control over your personal information and ensure that Eventh uses your data only in ways you have consented to.

    8. Children's Privacy

    Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect, store, or process information from minors. If we discover that personal data has been provided by a child under 13, we promptly remove it from our systems. Protecting children's privacy is important to us, and we comply with all applicable laws regarding minors. Parents or guardians are encouraged to contact us if they believe their child’s information has been collected so that we can take immediate action to ensure safety and privacy for young users.

    9. Policy Updates

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in law, technology, or our business practices. Any updates will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review the policy periodically to stay informed about how your data is handled. By regularly checking this page, you ensure that you are aware of any modifications that may affect your rights or the way your personal information is used. Staying informed allows you to continue using our services with full knowledge of our privacy practices.

    10. Contact Us

    If you have any questions regarding our privacy practices or how your personal information is handled, please do not hesitate to contact us. Our team is happy to assist you with inquiries, requests, or any concerns regarding your data. We aim to respond promptly and provide clear guidance on how your personal information is managed, ensuring transparency, trust, and confidence in Eventh’s privacy practices.
